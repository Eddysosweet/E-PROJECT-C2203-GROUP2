ONLINE IT TRAINING PROGRAM FOR WOMEN

With the mission of providing and training high-quality IT human resources for major global projects, UNICA offers unlimited career opportunities and a professional training environment according to international standards. UNICA is the starting place for the success of future IT engineers.

UNICA's TRAINING MODEL

For all subjects

The training courses are designed for everyone, including those who know nothing about IT. Flexible training time (Fulltime/Parttime) suitable for working people.

Real battle with experts

Students are involved in practice for more than 60% of the course time at Labs. The teaching staff are Technology experts with many years of experience in handling large-scale projects at UNICA.

Training methods

Student-centered, using a system of international standard learning materials and modern training techniques, building knowledge from basic to advanced, helping students to be fully equipped in the Technology industry.

Infrastructure

Students at UNICA will experience and use international standard lecture halls and offices at the most beautiful and modern campuses in Vietnam.

Certificates and job commitments

The certificate is issued by UNICA upon completion of the course. For some long-term courses, we are committed to arranging official positions for students with academic results of grade B or higher (7/10).

Professional environment

Working environment meets international standards, offices are present around the world (Vietnam, USA, Australia, Japan, Singapore, Germany, France...)
